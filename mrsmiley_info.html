Mr Smiley's World :D

  • Instructions
    • You control Mr. Smiley as he walks around his amazing world.
    • Use "a" to move left and "s" to move right
    • Use "g" to toggle simple animations to a variety of items in Mr Smiley's world
    • Use "r" to reset the world to the origin
